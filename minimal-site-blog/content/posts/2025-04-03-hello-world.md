## Why this exists

This site is **blog-first**: a quiet place for essays and technical notes. The full visual portfolio with project deep-dives lives at [tmarhguy.com](https://tmarhguy.com); here you get Markdown posts, RSS, and a sidebar with the essentials about me.

## What I write about

Expect RTL and physical design, FPGA bring-up, verification stories, and occasionally backend or tooling when it intersects a hardware project. If it runs on silicon or next to it, it is fair game.
